About Me

Learn about my journey, philosophy, and approach to holistic development

Hello, I'm Shruthi GK

With over 12 years of experience in yoga instruction and life skills coaching, I've dedicated my career to empowering individuals of all ages to unlock their full potential.

My journey began as a school teacher where I noticed a gap in emotional intelligence and communication skills among students. This inspired me to create a holistic approach that combines traditional yoga practices with modern life skills training.

I hold certifications in Hatha Yoga (RYT 500), Child Psychology, and Public Speaking from internationally recognized institutions. My unique methodology focuses on creating a nurturing environment where children and adults alike can develop confidence, emotional resilience, and effective communication skills.

What sets my approach apart is the integration of mindfulness practices with practical life skills. Through carefully designed programs, participants learn to manage stress, express themselves effectively, and build meaningful relationships.
